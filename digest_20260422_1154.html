MEDIUM REACH Science Magazine Podcast: The normals | Episode 3
Sarah Crespi · Apr 21 · 33 min

This episode examines the history and current state of healthy human subjects in clinical research, tracing the evolution from NIH's 'Normals' program (which recruited religious conscientious objectors in the 1950s) to today's privatized clinical trial system.
