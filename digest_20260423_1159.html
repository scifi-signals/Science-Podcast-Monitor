MEDIUM REACH Science Friday: Listening for the cosmic ‘dark ages,’ from the lunar far side
Ira Flatow · Apr 23 · 13 min

This episode features Anja Slosar discussing the Lucy Knight mission, a small pathfinder radio telescope being deployed to the lunar far side via NASA's Commercial Lunar Payload Services program. The instrument aims to observe extremely low radio frequencies (below 50 MHz) that are difficult to detect from Earth, potentially paving the way for future observations of the 'Dark Ages'—an early epoch of the universe before stars formed. While Lucy Knight itself will not detect Dark Ages signals, it will demonstrate the feasibility of the lunar far side as a premier radio observation site and help identify technical challenges for larger future instruments.
